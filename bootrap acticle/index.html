Elon Musk - business magnate, investor, engineer and inventor.

Here's a time line of Elon Musk's life:

  • 1971 - Born on June 28 in Pretoria, Transvaal, South Africa.
  • 1990 - He was accepted into Queen's University in Kingston, Ontario, for undergraduate study.
  • 1992 - After spending two years at Queen's University, he transferred to the University of Pennsylvania.
  • 1995 - Musk and his brother, Kimbal, started Zip2, a web software company, with money raised from a small group of angel investors.
  • 1997 - Received a Bachelor of Science degree in physics from its College of Arts and Sciences, and a Bachelor of Science degree in economics from its Wharton School of Business.
  • 1999 - Musk received US$22 million for his 7 percent share from the sale and co-founded X.com, an online financial services and e-mail payment company.
  • 2002 - Musk founded Space Exploration Technologies, or SpaceX, in May.
  • 2002 - PayPal was acquired by eBay for US$1.5 billion in stock, of which Musk received US$165 million.
  • 2004 - Musk led the Series A round of investment in February 2004, joining Tesla's board of directors as its chairman.[94] Musk took an active role within the company and oversaw Roadster product design at a detailed level, but was not deeply involved in day-to-day business operations.
  • 2006 - Musk provided the initial concept and financial capital for SolarCity, which was then co-founded by his cousins Lyndon and Peter Rive.
  • 2008 - Tesla Motors first built an electric sports car, the Tesla Roadster.
  • 2012 - Tesla began delivery of its four-door Model S sedan and unveiled its third product, the Model X.
  • 2013 - Musk unveiled a concept for a high-speed transportation system incorporating reduced-pressure tubes in which pressurized capsules ride on an air cushion driven by linear induction motors and air compressors.
  • 2015 - Musk announced the creation of OpenAI, a not-for-profit artificial intelligence (AI) research company. OpenAI aims to develop artificial general intelligence in a way that is safe and beneficial to humanity.
  • 2016 - Musk co-founded Neuralink, a neurotechnology startup company, to integrate the human brain with artificial intelligence.
  • 2016 - The Boring Company was founded after Elon Musk mentioned the idea of making tunnels on his Twitter account.
  • 2018 - Starman was launched into orbit of Mars on the Falcon Heavy rocket.
